Upcoming Events for the Two Hearts Project

First Friday & First Saturday Vigil - Holy Cross

Date: November 1-2, 2024

Location: Holy Cross Church, 123 Main St, North Carolina

Time: Vigil begins at 7:00 PM on November 1st and ends at 10:00 AM on November 2nd.

Join us for the First Friday and First Saturday devotion, beginning with Mass at 7:00 PM. Confession is available after Mass. The vigil continues through the night with adoration, rosary prayers, and silent reflection.

Two Hearts Retreat - Our Lady of Grace

Date: December 15, 2024

Location: Our Lady of Grace, 456 Church Ave, North Carolina

Time: 9:00 AM - 3:00 PM

This retreat will focus on deepening devotion to the Sacred Heart of Jesus and the Immaculate Heart of Mary. The day will include talks, prayer time, and a special Mass at noon. Lunch will be provided.

Advent Rosary & Vigil

Date: December 22, 2024

Location: Our Lady of the Rosary, 789 Cathedral Ln, North Carolina

Time: Begins at 7:00 PM

Join us as we prepare for the coming of Christ during Advent with a rosary and vigil. This event will include Scripture readings, hymns, and a candlelit rosary procession.
