The Two Hearts Devotion

What is the Two Hearts Devotion?

The Two Hearts Devotion is a powerful and beloved practice in the Catholic Church, focusing on the deep and unbreakable connection between the Sacred Heart of Jesus and the Immaculate Heart of Mary. This devotion encourages the faithful to reflect on the love, compassion, and mercy represented by the hearts of Jesus and Mary, and to offer prayers and sacrifices in reparation for sins and for the conversion of sinners.

The Sacred Heart of Jesus

The Sacred Heart of Jesus is a representation of Christâ€™s love for humanity, symbolized by His heart, often depicted as surrounded by thorns, pierced, and burning with love. Devotion to the Sacred Heart began in the 17th century after the visions of St. Margaret Mary Alacoque, who was given the mission to spread this devotion to the world. It reminds the faithful of Christ's infinite love, mercy, and willingness to forgive.

The Immaculate Heart of Mary

The Immaculate Heart of Mary symbolizes her pure, sinless heart, which was completely dedicated to the will of God. This devotion grew in prominence especially after the apparitions of Our Lady of Fatima, where Mary requested the consecration of the world to her Immaculate Heart. Devotion to her heart calls for consecration, prayer, and a life of purity and love, echoing Mary's perfect obedience to Gods plan.

How to Practice the Two Hearts Devotion

  • Attend Mass and offer Holy Communion in reparation to the Sacred Heart of Jesus and the Immaculate Heart of Mary.
  • Pray the Rosary daily, especially the First Five Saturdays devotion as Our Lady of Fatima requested.
  • Offer acts of consecration to the Sacred Heart of Jesus and the Immaculate Heart of Mary.
  • Spend time in adoration before the Blessed Sacrament, reflecting on the love and mercy of Jesus and Mary.
  • Live a life of charity, compassion, and forgiveness, modeling your heart after Jesus and Mary.

Prayers for the Two Hearts Devotion

Act of Consecration to the Sacred Heart of Jesus

"O Sacred Heart of Jesus, I consecrate myself to Your most Sacred Heart. Take possession of my whole being; transform me into Yourself. Make my hands Your hands, my feet Your feet, my heart Your heart. Let me see with Your eyes, listen with Your ears, speak with Your lips, love with Your heart, understand with Your mind, serve with Your will, and be dedicated with my whole being. Make me Your other self. Amen."

Act of Consecration to the Immaculate Heart of Mary

"O Immaculate Heart of Mary, I consecrate myself to your maternal love and protection. Guide my thoughts, words, and actions, so that they may always glorify God. Help me to grow in holiness, and bring me ever closer to the Sacred Heart of your Son, Jesus Christ. Amen."
